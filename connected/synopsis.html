END

narrator: Social media has its pros and cons. Lately, there seems to have been a lot of backlash on social media and a drive for people to delete their accounts and stay offline. I agree and do a hard disconnect now and then. However, I do believe that the ability to stay connected with the few people that I truly care about outweighs the cons. It is up to us on how we use social media-- it is a tool that helps shape society but also is shaped by the communities that use it in return.

How do you stay connected? Do you consider both the pros and cons specific to this age of new media? And if you are a fellow designer or creator in this space, how does your work encourage true connection between real people?
