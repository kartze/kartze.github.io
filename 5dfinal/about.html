This is an interactive short film exploring human connection through social media.

Elements in the film include spoofs of Gmail, GChat, and Facebook Messenger, in which connections--or the lack of connection-- is exaggerated.

Between scenes, you have mouse and limited keyboard control of the narrator's desktop to explore and influence the next scene.
