Short Film Fest 2023

Sunday nights | Oct 15 to Nov 19, 2023

A weekly gathering to watch short films

Oct 15 - Opening Night | RSVP

Let's kick it off! We'll watch films across a variety of genres and styles, as an introduction.

Oct 22 - Narrative

Short films have the difficult task of telling a story in just a few minutes. Come watch these stories unfold.

Oct 29 - Craft

Let's appreciate the different styles of film: live-action, animation, stop-motion, mixed media, and more.

Nov 5 - Experimental

Moving away from crowd-pleasers, let's dive into some less conventional films.

Nov 12 - Interactive

We've been sitting back and watching, but thanks to new media, we can more actively engage with short films. Let's play!

Nov 19 - Finale

We'll reflect on our favorites and wrap up with some final films.

hosted by Karisa
