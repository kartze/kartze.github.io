About the community

I'm Karisa, and I started this for fun.

In 2018, I hosted casual film nights at Northeastern University. In 2020— who would've seen this coming?— COVID-19 caused many events to move online. It prompted me to bring back my film fests as a way to bridge people during social isolation, and this time, in a new, virtual format.

This community is for everyone— the curious, enthusiasts, makers, and more.
